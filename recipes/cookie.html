Cookie Noonie

Description

There's nothing like walking into Grandma's house and being greeted by the sweet scent of freshly baked cookies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup shortening
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup dark molasses

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Sift flour, ginger, baking soda, cinnamon
  3. Place shortening in a mixing bowl and beat until creamy
  4. Pinch off small amounts of dough
