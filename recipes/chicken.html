Fried Chicken

Description

Always a hit! People will be wanting seconds. Crispy chicken that is made lower in fat, without being soggy.
You may remove the skin from the chicken if you like. Serve with Cheese Mashed Potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups low fat sour cream
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 6-bone-in chicken breast halves, with skin
  • 4 cups crushed cornflakes cereal
  • 4 tablespoons crushed dried rosemary
  • 3 teaspoons dried sage
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together sour cream and minced garlic
  3. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven
  4. Invite friends and family and ask them to pay for the dinner
